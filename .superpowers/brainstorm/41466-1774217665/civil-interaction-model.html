How does Civil live on the site?

Pick the interaction model that feels right. This shapes everything else.

A

💬 Floating Chat Bubble

Civil lives in the corner as a persistent chat widget. Users can open it anytime to ask questions, get help finding their reps, or draft a message. Conversational, always available.

Like Intercom — but it's a civic AI, not support

B

✍️ Inline Email Assistant

Civil appears inside the email compose modal. After you pick a rep, Civil's there to help you write — asks what your issue is, suggests tone, drafts the message. Contextual, focused.

Builds on the existing AI draft feature (currently disabled)

C

🧭 Guided Flow (wizard)

Civil walks users through the whole process: What's your issue? → Here's who to contact → Here's a draft. A step-by-step guided experience, not a freeform chat.

Most structured — best for first-time civic actors

Note from the Psychologist 🧠

"Civic participation anxiety is real — people feel their voice doesn't matter. The interaction model should reduce that activation energy, not add steps. The right choice here depends on where users drop off most."
