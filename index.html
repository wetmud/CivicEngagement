About CivicConnect

A free civic engagement tool for Canadians

I built CivicConnect because I got tired of not knowing who actually represents me. City councillor, regional rep, MPP — they're all out there, but finding them and reaching out felt harder than it should be. This tool fixes that.

Enter your address and instantly see every elected official who represents you, get a map of your ward, explore your city's budget in plain English, and draft a real email to your rep — all in under two minutes, with zero data collected about you.

It's free, open-source, and built to stay that way. If you find it useful, consider buying me a coffee — it helps cover the small API costs and keeps me motivated to expand coverage beyond Ontario.

Built by a Canadian who wanted their city to work better. Currently covering 8 Ontario cities, with more on the way.
